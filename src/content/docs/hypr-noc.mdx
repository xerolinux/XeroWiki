## What's this?

**HyprNoc** is a script that makes it easy to install **[Hyprland](https://wiki.hypr.land)** paired with the **[Noctalia Shell](https://noctalia.dev)** in a completely **Vanilla State**. That means nothing is pre-configured out of the box. No dotfiles, no tweaks, no opinions forced on you.

![Noctalia](../../assets/images/Noctalia.png)

What you get is a clean, working foundation. What you do with it is entirely up to you. Configure it from scratch, pull in pre-existing configs, apply community rices, or use bots and setup scripts from the **[Hyprland](https://wiki.hypr.land)** ecosystem. The script does not get in your way.

This is the simplest path to getting **[Hyprland](https://wiki.hypr.land)** and **[Noctalia](https://noctalia.dev)** up and running while keeping full control over your setup.

---

## Why Hyprland?

Here at **XeroLinux HQ**, we chose **[Hyprland](https://wiki.hypr.land)** because we consider it the most mature and feature-rich Wayland compositor available right now. It has a well-established ecosystem, active development, and solid community support that makes it a strong default choice.

That said, it is a choice, not a rule. **[Noctalia Shell](https://noctalia.dev)** is not locked to **[Hyprland](https://wiki.hypr.land)**. If you prefer a different window manager, there are plenty of great options out there to pair it with. Pick what fits your workflow and your taste.

## Installation

:::caution
This script is offered as-is, we will not be held responsible for any issues. It's been thoroughly tested on **XeroLinux**. However we do accept new ideas and suggestions on [Community Discord](https://discord.xerolinux.xyz).
:::

```bash
bash <(curl -fsSL https://xerolinux.xyz/script/hyprnoc.sh)
```

P.S : Qt/GTK style settings will affect KDE too. Revert them or use our **Reset** switch if you decide to go back to Plasma.
